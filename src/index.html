About Us

Elite Earthworks and Engineering is a Southern California Grading, excavating, and demolition contractor with a reputation of delivering projects to our clients with the highest quality of workmanship. We pride ourselves on delivering our work on time and within budget. We were founded in 1989 and have remained strong through the years by satisfying loyal customers. Whether you require comprehensive site preparation, intricate utility installations, or precision concrete and asphalt paving preparation, Elite Earthworks is your trusted partner. Our expertise across a wide spectrum of construction services ensures we can tackle any project, big or small.

Giving back To Uganda

Our construction company is committed to giving back to Uganda by actively participating in community development initiatives. Through partnerships with local organizations, we aim to contribute to the well-being of the Ugandan people. This includes supporting infrastructure projects, providing training opportunities for local residents, and promoting sustainable practices in the construction industry. By investing in the community, we strive to create a positive impact, foster growth, and enhance the overall quality of life for the people of Uganda.
