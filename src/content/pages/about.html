Why Choose Elite?

Customers should choose us because Elite offers value engineering to help our customers have a successful, cost-efficient project. The company's extensive years of experience and skillful staff assures that every project is handled professionally and with the highest level of attention. We also use the latest software and equipment to get our customers jobs bid and completed with unparalleled accuracy. This technology allows our estimators to compile the best possible budgets and proposals for our customers.

Our Story

Elite Earthworks and Engineerings started out as GK Backlund with Andrew Backlund. He has been in the Dirt business for most of his professional career and decided in 1989 to start his own business as an Owner. In 2011, GK Backlund became Incorporated, and in 2013 changed the name from GK Backlund to Elite Earthworks and Engineering.

Our Technology

Utilizing 3D modeling and GPS machine control, we deliver our work with utmost precision. The technology also gives our project managers additional tools to keep our customers informed on the current status of their project. Using our modern Field Tracking software system has given us an advantage of getting 'day of ' cost information on a job that gets passed back to estimating systems for better precise accuracy bidding information that will save our customers money.

Giving back To Uganda

Our construction company is committed to giving back to Uganda by actively participating in community development initiatives. Through partnerships with local organizations, we aim to contribute to the well-being of the Ugandan people. This includes supporting infrastructure projects, providing training opportunities for local residents, and promoting sustainable practices in the construction industry. By investing in the community, we strive to create a positive impact, foster growth, and enhance the overall quality of life for the people of Uganda.
