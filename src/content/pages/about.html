Why Choose Elite?

Customers should choose us because Elite offers value engineering to help our customers have a successful, cost-efficient project. The company's extensive years of experience and skillful staff assures that every project is handled professionally and with the highest level of attention. We also use the latest software and equipment to get our customers jobs bid and completed with unparalleled accuracy. This technology allows our estimators to compile the best possible budgets and proposals for our customers.

Our Story

Elite Earthworks and Engineerings started out as GK Backlund with Andrew Backlund. He has been in the Dirt business for most of his professional career and decided in 1989 to start his own business as an Owner. In 2011, GK Backlund became Incorporated, and in 2013 changed the name from GK Backlund to Elite Earthworks and Engineering.
