What is Public Works?

Public works encompass a vast array of construction projects designed to enhance the quality of life for communities at large. These projects are financed by public funds and play a pivotal role in creating the foundation for a thriving society. Public works include the construction and maintenance of roads, bridges, schools, hospitals, utilities, and various other infrastructural elements that contribute to the well-being and progress of a community.
